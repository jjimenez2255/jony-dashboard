30-Day Outreach Challenge

3 messages a day. Every day.

Messages Sent

Goal: 90 in 30 days
0 / 90
The Notice
"Saw your [thing]. [One specific observation]. Respect."
The Share
"This made me think of you" + link/insight/idea. No ask.
The Direct
"You have [problem]. I fix that. Worth a conversation?"

Weeks 1-2: Mostly Notices and Shares. Build warmth. Reconnect.
Weeks 3-4: Mix in Directs. Convert warmth to conversations.
The rule: Never pitch in the first message. Lead with them, not you.

The Physics

Laws that govern everything.

Law 1

Revenue = People x Price x Frequency

Only three levers. Everything else is noise.

Law 2

Value = Pain Solved

If they won't pay to make it stop today, it's not painful enough.

Law 3

Market Cap = Revenue x Multiple

Services: 1-3x. SaaS: 10-20x. Platform: 20-50x+.

Law 4

Decouple Revenue from Time

Hours = capped. Software = uncapped. Marketplace = exponential.

Law 5

Speed = Distance to Transaction

Fewer steps between "found you" and "paid you" = faster revenue.

The Filter

"Would I do this for equity?"

Yes = pursue. No = hand to NEWW team or kill.
