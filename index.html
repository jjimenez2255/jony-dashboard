The Shift

From attraction to conviction.

"If I have to go to them, my work isn't good enough."
→
"They don't know I exist yet. That's a distribution problem, not a value problem."
"Reaching out is desperate."
→
"Not telling them I can fix their problem is selfish."
"Build it and they'll come."
→
"Build it and carry it to the people who need it."

The Physics

Laws that govern everything.

Law 1

Revenue = People x Price x Frequency

Only three levers.

Law 2

Value = Pain Solved

If they won't pay to stop it, it's not painful enough.

Law 3

Market Cap = Revenue x Multiple

Services 1-3x. SaaS 10-20x. Platform 20-50x+.

Law 4

Decouple Revenue from Time

Hours = capped. Software = uncapped.

Law 5

Speed = Distance to Transaction

Fewer steps = faster revenue.

The Filter

"Would I do this for equity?"

Yes = pursue. No = hand off or kill.
